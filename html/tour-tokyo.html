Tokyo City Lights

Neon streets, luxury shopping, and timeless culture — Tokyo redefines modern Japan.

Experience the vibrant energy of Tokyo, where ancient traditions meet cutting-edge technology. From the bustling streets of Shibuya to the serene temples of Asakusa, this comprehensive tour takes you through the heart of Japan's capital city. Discover hidden gems, savor authentic cuisine, and immerse yourself in the unique culture that makes Tokyo one of the world's most captivating destinations.

Tour Itinerary

1

Arrival & Shibuya Exploration

  • Welcome meeting at hotel with tour guide
  • Visit Shibuya Crossing - world's busiest intersection
  • Explore Hachiko Square and Shibuya Center Street
  • Traditional Japanese lunch at local restaurant
  • Evening shopping at Shibuya 109 and Tokyu Hands
  • Dinner at authentic izakaya (Japanese pub)
2

Traditional Tokyo & Modern Districts

  • Morning visit to Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa
  • Stroll through Nakamise Shopping Street
  • Tokyo Skytree observation deck (optional upgrade)
  • Traditional tea ceremony experience
  • Explore Akihabara Electric Town
  • Dinner in Ginza district
3

Imperial Palace & Cultural Sites

  • Guided tour of Imperial Palace East Gardens
  • Visit Tokyo National Museum
  • Traditional Japanese lunch with kaiseki course
  • Explore Ueno Park and Ameya Yokocho Market
  • Evening walk through Roppongi Hills
  • Farewell dinner with local specialties
