Kyoto Heritage Escape

Ancient temples, tea gardens, and bamboo trails — serenity preserved in time

Step back in time and discover the ancient capital of Japan. Kyoto, with its over 2,000 temples and shrines, traditional wooden houses, and meticulously maintained gardens, offers a serene escape from the modern world. This carefully curated tour takes you through the most beautiful and historically significant sites, allowing you to experience the authentic Japanese culture, Zen philosophy, and the tranquil beauty that has inspired artists and poets for centuries.

Tour Itinerary

1

Temples & Traditional Districts

  • Welcome meeting with cultural guide
  • Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine - thousands of torii gates
  • Explore Gion district - Kyoto's geisha district
  • Traditional Japanese lunch at local restaurant
  • Visit Kiyomizu-dera Temple - wooden temple complex
  • Evening stroll through historic Higashiyama district
2

Bamboo Grove & Zen Gardens

  • Morning visit to Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Explore Tenryu-ji Temple and its Zen garden
  • Traditional tea ceremony experience
  • Visit Ryoan-ji Temple - famous rock garden
  • Lunch featuring Kyoto-style kaiseki cuisine
  • Explore Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji)
3

Cultural Immersion & Gardens

  • Visit Nijo Castle - UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Explore traditional Japanese gardens
  • Experience traditional kimono wearing (optional)
  • Visit Silver Pavilion (Ginkaku-ji)
  • Traditional multi-course kaiseki dinner
  • Evening cultural performance (optional)
