Osaka Food Adventure

From Dotonbori delights to Michelin dining — explore Japan’s flavorful heart.

Embark on a culinary journey through Osaka, Japan's food capital. Known as the "Kitchen of Japan," Osaka offers an incredible variety of street food, traditional dishes, and world-class dining experiences. From the vibrant neon lights of Dotonbori to hidden local izakayas, this food-focused tour will introduce you to the authentic flavors that make Osaka a food lover's paradise. Discover why locals say "kuidaore" (eat until you drop) when visiting this delicious city.

Tour Itinerary

1

Dotonbori Street Food Exploration

  • Welcome meeting with local food guide
  • Explore Dotonbori district - Osaka's food paradise
  • Sample takoyaki (octopus balls) from famous vendors
  • Try okonomiyaki (Japanese savory pancakes)
  • Visit Kuromon Ichiba Market - "Osaka's Kitchen"
  • Evening dinner at traditional kushikatsu restaurant
2

Cooking Class & Local Markets

  • Hands-on Japanese cooking class with local chef
  • Learn to make traditional Osaka dishes
  • Visit local fish market for fresh ingredients
  • Lunch featuring your prepared dishes
  • Explore Shinsekai district and try kushikatsu
  • Evening food tour of hidden local izakayas
3

Fine Dining & Cultural Sights

  • Morning visit to Osaka Castle
  • Traditional kaiseki lunch experience
  • Explore Umeda Sky Building
  • Afternoon tea at traditional tea house
  • Michelin-starred restaurant dinner (optional upgrade)
  • Farewell night walk through Dotonbori
